My Journey

My journey into Traditional Chinese Medicine began over 13 years ago in a Buddhist temple, where I studied acupuncture under my master while helping provide free, charitable treatments to members of the local community. Witnessing people experience relief from pain and improvements in their health through compassionate care inspired me to dedicate my career to helping others.

To further develop my knowledge and clinical skills, I completed formal studies in Traditional Chinese Medicine and Acupuncture at Endeavour College of Natural Health between 2013 and 2017. Following graduation, I travelled throughout Asia, volunteering in hospitals within Traditional Chinese Medicine departments. Working alongside healthcare professionals in hospital settings gave me valuable insight into how Traditional Chinese Medicine can complement conventional medical care, and strengthened my appreciation for combining traditional practices with a modern understanding of health.

In 2019, I established my own clinic in Cabramatta as a registered practitioner. Since then, I have continued to expand my practice while also working alongside multidisciplinary healthcare professionals, including physiotherapists and chiropractors. These experiences have allowed me to care for patients from diverse backgrounds and with a wide range of health concerns. My approach to treatment extends beyond relieving symptoms. I believe that good healthcare begins with understanding your own body. I take the time to listen to each patient's concerns, explain what their symptoms may be communicating, and ensure they understand both their condition and the treatment process. I aim to make Traditional Chinese Medicine approachable and accessible by helping patients understand how acupuncture and other natural therapies support the body's ability to restore balance.

Alongside treatments such as acupuncture, cupping, moxibustion, gua sha, and tuina massage, I encourage patients to improve their long-term wellbeing through practical lifestyle changes, including healthy nutrition, balanced daily routines, gentle exercise such as Tai Chi, and other holistic practices. My goal is not only to help patients feel better today, but to give them the knowledge and confidence to take an active role in their own health for years to come.
